~Blockhead~

Benny's Adventure

A live, one woman solo performance, in which a 3D cube comes to in a world unknown. Vaporwave and good vibes to follow.

-Computer Vision -QR Tracking -Processing/Java -Photoshop -Illustrator

Video of the performance.



Behind the Scenes

This project was my first live performance piece.

Using unique QR codes on ever corner of the box, I was able to map out a square onto the block on my head. Through the QR codes and a camera, I was able to use computer vision to track my movements. I was then able to project various faces and expressions to create my character of blockhead.

The performance was only about a minute in length, but the mix of visuals, music, and storytelling was very fun to work with!
